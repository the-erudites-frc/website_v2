Welcome ...

Welcome to The Erudites! We are a passionate, community-based, student-led robotics team dedicated to inspiring the next generation of innovators and leaders! We operate under the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) framework, which challenges high school students to build and program industrial-sized robots to compete in thrilling, themed challenges.

Our mission is to cultivate STEM skills, teamwork, and leadership through hands-on experience and community engagement. We believe in the power of collaboration and strive to create an inclusive environment where every member can thrive, learn, and contribute.

What We Do ...

  • Robot Building and Programming: Each season, our team designs, builds, and programs a robot to meet specific game requirements, pushing the boundaries of creativity and engineering.
  • Community Outreach: We actively engage with our local community through workshops, demonstrations, and mentoring programs to spread the excitement of STEM and robotics.
  • Competitions: We compete in regional and national FRC competitions, showcasing our hard work and sportsmanship while learning from other teams.
